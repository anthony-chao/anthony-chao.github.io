NetworkIn

Project Info:

NetworkIn is a clone of LinkedIn - the largest professional network on the internet where professionals can connect, share and learn. Users have the ability to create a professsional profile, create/edit/delete posts, add comments/likes, and connect with other users!

Project Details:

  • Technologies:Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, React, Redux, JavaScript
  • URL:https://github.com/anthony-chao/NetworkIn
